GURUGRAM STREET FOOD

1. Bombay Best Paav-Bhaji (Sector 4)

This small stall in sector 4 Gurgaon is winning the heart of thousands of locals in Gurgaon. Bombay Best Paav Bhaji serves the delicious and tasty pav bhaji. They use the fresh paav to make their Paav Bhaji and serve with hot and spicy sabji. A must try to all street food lovers 

    Service options: Dine-in · Takeaway · Delivery
    Address: Shop no. 29 near domino's sec-4 huda market,
     Gurugram, Haryana 122001
    Hours: 2pm - 11pm everyday
    Phone: 09810392495

2. Pappu Fish Corner – New Colony of Old Gurgaon

Pappu Fish Corner is situated in New Colony of Old Gurgaon. They are in business for the last 33 years. They sell fish tandooris. People always hover over this stall for delicious food. The fish tandoori is served with Surmai fish. It is served with spicy masalas and drops of lemon juice. 

    Service options: Dine-in · Drive-through · Delivery
    Address: near Raj Mahal Hotel, More, Rattan Garden, 
    Shivpuri Extension, 
    Sector 7, Gurugram, Haryana 122001
    Hours: 5pm-10:30pm everyday
    Phone: 098918 57278

3. Sardaar Jalebi Vaala (Sadar Bazaar)

The 68-year-old sweet shop in Sadar Bazar is thronged with hundreds of customers almost every day. Many News channels covered them and a family is running this shop from when India and Pakistan divided. 

Service options: In-store shopping · Delivery
Address: F26J+R7H, Jacobpura Rd, Gopal Nagar, Adarsh Nagar, 
Roshan Pura, Gurugram, Haryana 122001
Hours:9am - 10 pm

4. Delhi Chaat Bhandaar

One of the best street food in Gurgaon. A must visit the shop for Dahi Bhalle, Aloo Chaat, and Gol Gappe. Very popular among the foodies and local audiences. Sometimes you have to wait to get your turn. 

    Service options: Dine-in · Takeaway · Delivery
    Address: H. No. 601/13,Nai Basti, Opposite Kabir Bhawan,
    Old Railway Rd, Pratap Nagar, Sector 8, Gurugram, Haryana 122001
    Hours 2:30pm - 10:30pm
    Phone: 9891075340

5. Gandhi Pakore Wala – Sadar Bazar Gurgaon.

This is the place which is very famous in Gurgaon. Third generations of Gandhi family running this pakora shop. People love their Pakoras serve with chutneys and masalas. A must visit pakora shop for street food lovers. 

    Service options: Dine-in · Takeaway · Delivery
    Address: Sadar Bazar Rd, Sadar Bazar, Roshan Pura, 
    Gurugram, Haryana 122007
    Hours: 9am - 8pm
    Phone: 0124 232 8412
