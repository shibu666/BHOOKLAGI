DELHI STREET FOOD

1.Paratha, Moolchand

Their Aloo Pyaz Paratha and Egg Paratha are a must-try, especially when paired with a glass of Sweet or Mango Lassi. Located in the Moolchand metro station, the parathas here are worth every minute and penny you spend before boarding that last train. 

                    Service options: Dine-in · Takeaway · No-contact delivery
                    Address: 4, 5, 6 Lala Lajpat Rai Marg Moolchand Metro Station,
                    Lala Lajpat Rai Marg, Vikram Vihar, Lajpat Nagar, New Delhi, 
                    Delhi 110024
                    Hours: 8am - 11pm
                    Phone: 098717 34042

2.Bombay Pav Bhaji, Model Town

These guys serve two varieties of pav bhaji, a regular one and an extra buttery variant, that will make your cheat days totally worth it. This place is affordable and close to North Campus – two of the best reasons why you’ll see the eatery constantly cramped by young (and broke) students 

                        Service options: Dine-in · Drive-through · No-contact delivery
                        Address: D- 2/ 4, 3rd Main Rd, Model Town 3, Pocket D 2, Phase 2,
                        Model Town, Delhi, 110009
                        Hours:4:30pm - 10:30pm
                        Phone: 092129 74917

3.Roshan Di Kulfi, Karol Bagh

Thanks to their cult of kulfi lovers, Roshan Di Kulfi is known as one of the best street food places in Delhi. They serve some of the best chaats in town, but what they’re really known for is the iconic kulfi-falooda, Delhi’s answer to ice cream and gelato 

                        Service options: Dine-in · Takeaway · No-contact delivery
                        Address: 2816, Ajmal Khan Rd, Block 34P, Beadonpura, Karol Bagh,
                        New Delhi, Delhi 110005
                        Hours:8am - 10pm
                        Phone: 011 2872 4230

4.Ghalib Kabab Corner, Nizamuddin West

This eatery is open past midnight, but we suggest coming here early if you don’t want to miss out on their delish seekh kebabs, nihari and biryani. It’s located close to Hazrat Nizamuddin Dargah. No wonder their food is so heavenly. 

                    Service options: Dine-in · Drive-through · Delivery
                    Address: Markzi masjid, हज़रत निजामुल्मुल्क मागे, Nizamuddin, Nizamuddin-West,
                    New Delhi, Delhi 110013
                    Hours:12pm - 12am
                    Phone: 098107 86479

5.Prince Chaat Corner, M Block G

An affordable street food eatery in the posh GK locality, who would have thought? No wonder this place, serving some of the most delicious and hygienic dishes like Palak Patta Chaat, Bhalla Papri, Golgappe and Raj Kachori, is everyone’s favourite. They might be named Prince, but to us, they’re the king of Delhi chaats. 

        Service options: Dine-in · Takeaway · Delivery
        Address: M- 32, Greater Kailash 1 Rd, Greater Kailash-1, M Block, Greater Kailash I,
        Greater Kailash, New Delhi, Delhi 110048
        Hours:10am - 10pm

6.Bombay Bhel Puri, South Extension I

South Extension has some really cool joints if you’re on the lookout for street food in Delhi. And this outlet is proof enough. If you need a pick-me-up in between shopping but also don’t want to feel too full, their Sevpuri will “sev” the day. 

                    Service options: Dine-in · Takeaway · Delivery
                    Address: Near Midland Book Shop G 7, South Extension I, New Delhi, 
                    Delhi 110049
                    Hours:9am - 10pm
                    Phone: 098111 03083

7.Jain Chawal Wale, Connaught Place

If you happen to be in CP and are wondering where to go for some famous street food in Delhi, this is one place you defo need to check out. Try their Rajma Chawal, Shahi Paneer Chawal, Kadhi Chawal and the range of different parathas at less than 100 bucks. The place serves pure veg, but you won’t even be thinking about that chicken leg after a full meal here 

                        Service options: Dine-in · Drive-through · Delivery
                        Address: Baba Kharak Singh Rd, Block P, Connaught Place, New Delhi, 
                        Delhi 110001
                        Hours:5am - 12:30

8.Jung Bahadur Kachori, Wala Chandni Chowk

Your journey of discovering street food in old Delhi will be incomplete without a visit to this eatery in Chandni Chowk. This place is known for its mouthwatering gravy that is served on top of the crispy fried kachoris. Every bite feels like a warm hug, and the steaming kachoris will be your BFFs during the freezing Delhi winters. 

                        Service options: Drive-through · Delivery
                        Address: 1104, Gali Bhojpura Rd, Kinari Bazar, Maliwara, Katra Shah N Shah, 
                        Chandni Chowk, New Delhi, Delhi 110006
                        Hours:10:am - 7:30pm(closed on sunday)
                        Phone: 098916 98995

9.Qureshi Kabab, Corner Jama Masjid

This place has been in the kabab business for around 72 years. Yet, they remain a favourite even today. They even have a whole street dedicated to the family name in Old Delhi – Gali Kababian – a place you must not miss if you love food. Visit this place and name one variety of kabab you don’t like. We dare you. 

                        Service options: Dine-in · Drive-through · Delivery
                        Address: Gate no. 1, Near Jama Masjid, Urdu Bazar, 
                        New Delhi, Delhi 110006
                        (A 3-min walk from the Jama Masjid, Delhi)
                        Hours: 5pm - 12am
                        Phone: 097173 26041

10.AL Jawahar

Al Jawahar is legendary. It is said it was inaugurated by India’s first Prime Minister Jawaharlal Nehru. . Al Jawahar is a place is for Epicureans. It offers a culinary pleasure for foodies that can be compared to nothing else. While thinking of authentic Mughlai food, the foremost name that probably flashes across the minds of the people of Old Delhi region is the Mutton & Chicken delicacies served at AL Jawahar. Having established many years back, mutton stew is one of the popular delicacies here. This item is a slightly spicy with hint of masala to offer distinct taste. The meat is perfect cooked and it is so tender. You just crave more and more right the moment the gravy of meat stew enter your tongue. Sheermal is the sweet bread served here with the combination of stew that is simply fantastic . Chicken Mutton Korma,Chicken Jahangiri,Chicken Changezi with Khameeri Roti a deadly combo. There is no doubt on the quality of the food served here as they retain the genuine Mughlai taste 

                        Service options: Dine-in · Takeaway · No-contact delivery
                        Address: Shop no. 8, Jama Masjid, opposite Gate Number 1, Bazar Matia Mahal,
                         Kalan Mehal, Ansari Nagar East, New Delhi, Delhi 110006
                        A 2-min walk from the Jama Masjid, Delhi
                        Hours:9am - 12am
                        Phone: 098216 96018
